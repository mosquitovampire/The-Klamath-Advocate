SUPPORT GROUPS

There are currently two support groups. Family Support Group for those with an ill family member and Connections Peer Support Group for peers. Family Support Group meets once a month on the first tuesday of every month. Connections Peer Support Group meets twice a month on the 2nd and 4th tuesday of every month. TIME: Noon-1:30pm. Location: 2421 Washburn Way, Suite E (Sky Lakes Complex) One-On-One support is available. To schedule call 541-885-4909
